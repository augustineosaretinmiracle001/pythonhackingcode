Place the following images in this directory:

Required Images:
- favicon.ico (website icon)
- logo.png (main logo)
- slide1.jpg (hero banner image)
- partners-logo.png (partners banner)
- logof.png (Facebook logo for voting)
- logoi.png (Instagram logo for voting)

You can use placeholder images or create your own images for these files.

Image sizes recommended:
- logo.png: 200x100px
- slide1.jpg: 1200x400px
- partners-logo.png: 800x200px
- logof.png, logoi.png: 50x50px each
